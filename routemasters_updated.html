About Us

Driven by Purpose,
Powered by Experience

We started RouteMasters Pro because we believe every truck driver deserves a fair shot at maximizing their earnings without the headache of running a business alone.

From a Small Desk to a Nationwide Operation

Founded in 2018, RouteMasters Pro started with a simple mission: help owner-operators and small fleet owners get the support they need to compete with larger carriers. Our founders, having spent years in the logistics industry, understood the pain points firsthand — the endless broker calls, the paperwork burden, and the revenue left on the table.

Today, we've grown into a team of 40+ experienced dispatchers serving over 500 drivers across all 48 continental states. We've successfully dispatched more than 15,000 loads and helped our clients earn an average of 32% more per mile.

But we've never lost sight of what matters most: treating every driver like family and every load like it's our own business on the line.

Our Mission

To empower owner-operators and small fleet owners with world-class dispatching services that maximize revenue, minimize stress, and create a sustainable path to business growth in the trucking industry.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and driver-centric dispatch company in America, setting the industry standard for transparency, reliability, and results — where every driver feels like our only client.

Services for Every Need

Whether you're a solo owner-operator or managing a fleet, our comprehensive dispatch solutions are designed to grow with your business.

Load Booking

Our dispatchers scour top load boards and broker networks to find freight that pays what you deserve. We match loads to your equipment type, preferred lanes, and schedule — never forcing you into bad deals.

  • Access to 150+ broker networks
  • Rate negotiation on every load
  • Backhaul planning to reduce deadhead
  • Lane preference matching

Route Planning

We plan the most efficient routes factoring in distance, fuel costs, tolls, weather conditions, and delivery windows. Our goal is to get you there safely, on time, and profitably.

  • Fuel-optimized routing
  • Real-time weather monitoring
  • HOS compliance tracking
  • Toll and weigh station planning

Driver Support

When you're out on the road, things happen. Breakdowns, delays, confusing delivery instructions — we're your lifeline. Call us anytime and we'll sort it out.

  • Emergency breakdown coordination
  • Fuel stop recommendations
  • Receiver/broker communication
  • Detention & layover tracking

Documentation Handling

We manage all the paperwork that comes with every load — from rate confirmations to bills of lading, invoices, and proof of delivery. Nothing falls through the cracks.

  • Rate confirmation management
  • BOL collection & verification
  • Invoice generation & tracking
  • Organized digital filing system

24/7 Dispatch Services

The trucking industry doesn't follow a 9-to-5 schedule, and neither do we. Our dispatchers work in shifts to provide uninterrupted coverage — nights, weekends, and holidays included.

  • Round-the-clock availability
  • Holiday and weekend coverage
  • Quick response time (under 5 min)
